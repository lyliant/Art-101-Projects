- parent & child: Added space helmets, color variety, backpacks, caps, and sunglasses
- bounce: ltSpacePup moves side to side on the screen looking like a superhero
- wraparound: Makes ltSpacePup2 drop down from the screen
- wrapper/keypressed: this moves with space ship with the mouseX and mouseY. Uses r for rotating, t for scaling down,s for scaling up,m is for
translating on the x-axis, and n is for translating on the y-axis.
